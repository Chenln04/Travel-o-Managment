# 💡 Retrospective: Lessons Learned

Developing Travel-o provided critical insights into managing multidisciplinary tech projects involving AI and third-party integrations.

## 1. Strategic Planning
* **WBS Importance:** Defining a clear Work Breakdown Structure and dependencies was vital to avoid confusion and maintain the schedule.
* **Incremental AI Design:** Training AI models is an iterative process. Starting with a basic functional version before adding complexity is essential.

## 2. Technical Challenges
* **API Volatility:** External services are prone to changes and failures. Future iterations must prioritize robust fallback mechanisms and data normalization.
* **Data Consistency:** Unifying disparate data formats from multiple providers is a mandatory step for AI accuracy.

## 3. Team & Risk Management
* **Multidisciplinary Coordination:** Constant alignment between developers, designers, and product leads is the only way to achieve a cohesive product.
* **Risk Flexibility:** Project leads must be prepared to adjust plans in real-time when facing technical roadblocks like cloud costs or API limitations.

## 4. User-Centric Focus
* **Early Feedback:** Internal testing revealed that the AI assistant's tone must be concise and highly adaptable to user needs to be effective.
