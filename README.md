# ✈️ Travel-o: AI Startup Simulation
**Academic Project | Business Management | Multimedia Engineering (UPV)**

## 📌 Executive Summary
Travel-o is a fictitious startup concept developed as a technical and business simulation. The project focuses on an AI-powered platform designed to automate travel planning.

**My Role:** Project Coordinator

**Team:** 4 members

**Objective:** Deliver a high-fidelity prototype and a comprehensive business/technical report.

---

## 👔 Coordination & Leadership
In this project, I acted as the bridge between the technical requirements and the product vision. Even in an academic setting, I focused on:

* **Team Alignment:** Ensuring all 4 members understood the project scope and deadlines.
* **Requirements Gathering:** Translating the "startup idea" into technical features (AI integration, UI components).
* **Conflict Resolution:** Managing different opinions on design vs. functionality to meet the final submission date.

## 📂 Deliverables
* **[Comprehensive Project Report (PDF)](./Travel-o_FinalProject_Report.pdf):** The full document containing the business model, technical architecture, and UI/UX research.
* **Conceptual Roadmap:** The phases we followed from ideation to the final presentation.
